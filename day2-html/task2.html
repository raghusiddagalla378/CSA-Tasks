3. description lists

APPLE
Apples are healthy, tasty, and among the most popular fruits in the world. Although they are not particularly rich in vitamins and minerals, they're a good source of fibers and antioxidants. Apples may have several benefits, including improved heart health and a lower risk of cancer and diabetes.
GRAPES
Resveratrol is a key nutrient in grapes that may offer health benefits. Grapes are a good source of fiber, potassium, and a range of vitamins and other minerals. Grapes are suitable for people with diabetes as long as people include them in their diet plan
BANANA
A banana is a long, yellow fruit that is edible in either its raw or ripe form. We usually see bananas in a cluster, and it is a sweet fruit that children and adults enjoy eating.
MANGO
Mango is the national fruit of India which is loved by one and all. It is a very juicy, pulpy and luscious fruit. Ripe mangoes can either be consumed raw or in the form of salad, juice, jams, milkshake or pickles. Mango is a rich source of various vitamins and minerals.
